I'm a graduate student and researcher with a passion for educational innovation, especially what may drive the evolution of how we educate. I believe in equal access and opportunities in education for all students.

My journey began when I started my first education classes in undergrad. I completed a student teaching internship figuring out everything that helps run an elementary school. Since then, I've been focused on developing skills in curriculum design and working on projects that not only educate students, but make a positive impact on a school environment as a whole.

Currently, I'm creating various different modules and curriculums for class, while also focusing on the UX/UI of websites, and how they function. I'm particularly interested in elementary education, as I think that is the foundation for all learning, and love collaborating with others who share similar passions.

When I'm not working, you can find me at the pool table, or at a restaurant with delicious food. I'm always eager to learn new things and take on exciting challenges.
