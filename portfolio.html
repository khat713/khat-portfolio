HDFS Research Project

As the sole researcher, I designed and conducted a semester-long study on the effects of mindfulness exercises on elementary student behavior at Carrboro Elementary School. My responsibilities included creating and distributing Google Forms surveys, conducting interviews, collecting and organizing student data in spreadsheets, and presenting findings. This 335-hour project resulted in measurable reductions in student testing anxiety.
